FAQ

How do I generate a new quote?

There are several ways to get new quotes:

  • Click the "Generate New Quote" button for a random quote
  • Use the search bar to find quotes by keywords or authors
  • Click on category buttons like "Love", "Motivation", "Wisdom", etc.
  • Use the language selector to get quotes in different languages

How do I save and manage my favorite quotes?

Managing your favorite quotes is easy:

  • Click the heart icon (♥) to like a quote
  • Use the bookmark icon to save quotes for later
  • Your liked and saved quotes are stored locally on your device
  • You can unlike or unsave quotes by clicking the same buttons again

What languages are supported?

Currently, we support the following languages:

  • English (English quotes and interface)
  • हिंदी (Hindi quotes and interface)
  • 日本語 (Japanese quotes and interface)
  • नेपाली (Nepali quotes and interface)

To change the language, use the language selector in the top navigation bar.

How can I share quotes?

You have multiple sharing options:

  • Click the "Share" button to copy the quote to your clipboard
  • Use the "Download" button to save the quote as an image
  • Share directly to social media (if available on your device)
  • The shared quote will include both the text and author's name

What are the different categories available?

We offer a wide range of categories including:

  • Motivational and Inspirational quotes
  • Love, Friendship, and Relationships
  • Success and Leadership
  • Life, Wisdom, and Philosophy
  • Happiness and Peace
  • Dreams and Hope
  • Funny and Light-hearted quotes

Simply click on any category button to see quotes from that category.

How do I use the search feature?

The search feature is versatile and easy to use:

  • Type any keyword in the search bar
  • Search by quote content, author name, or category
  • Press Enter or click the search icon to search
  • Results will show matching quotes in your selected language
  • If no matches are found, try different keywords or check your spelling

How do I change the theme?

You can customize the app's appearance:

  • Find the theme switcher at the bottom right corner
  • Choose from Light, Dark, Sunset, Ocean, or Forest themes
  • Your theme preference will be saved for future visits
  • Each theme offers a unique color scheme and style
